# Wookie Cookie Shop

## Description
The Wookie Cookie Shop is an innovative online platform that allows customers to order delicious and unique cookies inspired by the beloved Wookiee characters from the Star Wars universe. The website offers various cookie flavors, customizable options, and a user-friendly shopping experience.

## Features
- **Wide Range of Cookie Flavors:** Choose from chocolate chip, peanut butter, oatmeal raisin, and more.
- **Customization:** Personalize your cookie order with your preferred ingredients.
- **User Accounts:** Create an account to save your favorite orders and track your deliveries.
- **Secure Payment Options:** Multiple payment methods for secure transactions.
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for both desktop and mobile devices.

## How to Use
1. **Visit the Website:** Go to the Wookie Cookie Shop homepage.
2. **Browse Flavors:** Explore available cookie flavors and customization options.
3. **Add to Cart:** Select your desired cookies and add them to your shopping cart.
4. **Checkout:** Complete the checkout process by providing your shipping information and payment details.
5. **Enjoy Your Cookies:** Wait for your cookies to be delivered to your doorstep!

## Project Structure
```
├── assets
│   ├── images
│   ├── styles
│   └── scripts
├── components
├── pages
│   ├── Home.vue
│   ├── Shop.vue
│   └── Checkout.vue
├── App.vue
└── main.js
```
- **assets/**: Contains images, styles, and scripts used in the project.
- **components/**: Reusable components utilized across pages.
- **pages/**: Contains the main views of the application such as Home, Shop, and Checkout.
- **App.vue**: The root component of the application.
- **main.js**: The entry point of the application where the app is initialized.
